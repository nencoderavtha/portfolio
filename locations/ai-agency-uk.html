Frequently Asked Questions

Can an Indian agency handle UK business operations?

Yes. Exter.ai is built to ship globally. We use international standards for coding, security, and data handling, ensuring our AI systems integrate flawlessly with western CRM and business tools.

What timezone does Exter.ai work in?

While our core development happens in IST, we hold client meetings and support sessions aligned with UK business hours. We rely on async Loom videos for daily progress updates so you never have to stay up late.

How does payment work for international clients?

We accept international wire transfers and secure credit card payments via Stripe in USD, GBP, AED, or AUD. Invoices are straightforward with zero hidden fees.

Who will I be working with?

You will work directly with Sathvik Yadala, our Founder & CEO, and our core engineering team. We do not use account managers to gatekeep communication.
