3D Website Design for Business

A 3D website is an immersive, interactive web experience that uses three-dimensional graphics, cinematic motion design, and scroll-driven animations to tell your brand story in a way flat websites cannot. Built with WebGL, Three.js, and GSAP, 3D websites create memorable first impressions that increase engagement, reduce bounce rates, and position your brand as an industry leader. Exter.ai builds custom 3D websites for businesses that need to look like they belong in the future — not the past.

How We Build Your 3D Website

01

02

Design

We create the visual direction — 3D scenes, motion concepts, color systems, and interaction patterns. Brand development included.

03

Develop

We build the 3D experience using WebGL, Three.js, and GSAP. Optimized for performance on every device. Working preview by day 10.

04

Launch

Testing, performance tuning, and deployment. You get full ownership of all code and assets. 30 days of free support.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a 3D website cost?

Costs vary based on complexity, number of 3D scenes, and interactivity level. We provide fixed-price proposals after a free discovery call — you'll know exactly what you're paying before work begins.

How long does it take to build?

Typical delivery is 3 weeks. You get a working preview by day 10. Highly complex projects may take 4–5 weeks.

Will it be slow on mobile?

No. We optimize 3D assets, implement progressive loading, and use GPU-accelerated rendering. Our sites target 90+ PageSpeed scores on mobile.

What technology do you use?

WebGL, Three.js, React Three Fiber, and GSAP for animations. All runs natively in the browser — no plugins needed.

Do you handle brand development too?

Yes. Brand development is included — visual identity, typography, color systems, and brand guidelines are delivered alongside the website.

Do I own the code?

Yes. Full ownership of all code, 3D assets, brand materials, and documentation. No vendor lock-in.
