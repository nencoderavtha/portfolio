FAQ

Questions we get asked.

What does Exter.ai do?

Exter.ai is an AI-native studio that builds websites, AI automations, AI receptionists, fullstack applications, and brand identities for ambitious founders. We use AI to ship faster and deliver enterprise-grade quality at startup speed, with fixed pricing and no pitch decks.

What is Exter AI?

Exter AI (also known as exter-ai, exter.ai, or ExterAI) is a disruptive, AI-native studio built by a team of aspiring engineers — based in India and shipping worldwide. We help startups and ambitious founders punch above their weight with AI receptionists, immersive 3D websites, fullstack development, and AI video branding: enterprise-grade craft delivered at startup speed.

How much does Exter.ai charge?

We provide fixed-price proposals after a free discovery call. Every project gets a written plan with scope, timeline, and exact price before we start. No hourly billing, no surprise invoices.

How long does a project take?

Typical timelines range from 4 days for an AI receptionist to 3 weeks for a full 3D website. We provide exact timelines in our proposal after the discovery call.

What is an AI receptionist?

An AI receptionist is a voice-enabled artificial intelligence system that answers phone calls, qualifies leads, books appointments, and handles customer inquiries 24/7. Exter.ai builds custom AI receptionists that integrate with CRM systems, calendar tools, and phone networks. Typical setup time is 4 days.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes. Exter.ai ships worldwide. We have clients across the US, UK, UAE, and Asia. We communicate in English, work across time zones, and handle everything from discovery to deployment remotely.
