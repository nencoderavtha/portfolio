CASE STUDY: AI RECEPTIONIST

Dental Clinic Voice AI.

The Challenge

A fast-growing dental practice was losing new patient leads because they couldn't answer calls during peak hours or after closing. Furthermore, a traditional receptionist's salary was eating up 10% of their total monthly revenue, yet they still couldn't offer 24/7 coverage. The doctor needed a solution that would streamline operations so they could manage the clinic seamlessly without bloated overhead.

The Solution

We built and deployed a custom AI Voice Receptionist trained exclusively on the clinic's services, pricing, and scheduling availability. The AI agent integrated directly into their booking system, answering every single call instantly with a natural, human-like voice. They became the very first clinic in their locality to implement this disruptive technology, immediately sparking a local craze and distinguishing their brand as hyper-modern.

The Financial Impact

By eliminating the traditional receptionist salary, the clinic immediately recouped 10% of their monthly revenue. Furthermore, the 24/7 availability led to a 15% increase in total bookings, translating to a substantial 10% boost in overall gross revenue. Previously, the clinic was operating at an average 60% profit margin (savings on revenue). With the 10% salary cost eliminated and the influx of new high-margin bookings, their net profit margin soared to an impressive 76%. The doctor now seamlessly manages the whole clinic with higher efficiency and significantly greater profitability.
